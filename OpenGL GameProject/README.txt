To control the Cube:
Use the WASD keys.

The camera follows the cube, behind it.
Use Keys E & Q to rotate.

Use key R to reload the terrain without having to relaunch the program.

Escape key can exit the program.

KNOWN "ISSUES":
After the first load time it takes 1 to 2 seconds for the particles to continually run.
On each sequential load, or any time further the particles continue to behave normally.
